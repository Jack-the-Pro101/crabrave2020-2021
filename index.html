About

Welcome! This site is a small project made by Jack H. to celebrate the ending of the year 2020. Since it wasn't the best year, the meme of crab rave here will be "2020 is gone!" Crabs start dancing. I have used JavaScript to make the Crab Rave music video start playing at an exact time so that when the beat drop hits, it will hit at 12:00AM. There are only 2 simple steps to do: 1. Play the video, 2. Pause the video and 3. drag the playhead to the beginning. Done! After this, keep the page open, sit back and relax! (Video demo under)
